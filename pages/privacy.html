Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

1. Introduction

LearnLu ("we," "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and use information when you visit our website.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect: email addresses (if you subscribe to our newsletter), usage data through analytics tools (pages visited, time on site), and cookies placed by Google AdSense and Google Analytics.

3. Google AdSense Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our site and other websites. You may opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ads Settings. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites on the Internet.

4. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This collects anonymous data such as pages visited, time on site, and general location (city/country level). No personal information is collected through Analytics.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies for analytics and advertising purposes. By using our site, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

6. Your Data Rights

You may request deletion of your personal data (email address) at any time by contacting us. Analytics data is anonymous and cannot be linked to you personally.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, visit our Contact page.

8. Google Consent Mode

We implement Google Consent Mode v2 on this website. This means that if you decline cookies, Google Analytics and Google AdSense will operate in a limited mode that does not store personal data or serve personalised ads. Your consent choice is saved in your browser and can be changed at any time by clearing your browser cookies.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to: access the personal data we hold about you, request deletion of your data, opt out of personalised advertising at Google Ad Settings, and withdraw consent at any time by clearing cookies in your browser settings.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at hello@learnlu.com.
