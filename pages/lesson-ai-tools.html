Introduction to AI Tools for Beginners: ChatGPT and More

The newest tools on the internet explained simply. What AI assistants are, what they can do, and what they can't.

What Is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to computer programs that can understand language, answer questions, write text, and perform tasks that previously required human intelligence. AI tools like ChatGPT, Google Gemini, and Claude can have conversations, explain complex topics, help with writing, and solve problems.

An AI assistant is like having a very knowledgeable colleague available 24/7 who has read millions of books and articles. You can ask it almost any question in plain English, and it gives a helpful response — like asking a friend who happens to know a lot about everything.

Using ChatGPT — Step by Step

1

Go to chat.openai.com

Create a free account with your email. ChatGPT (GPT-4o) is available for free.

2

Type your request in the chat box

Type a question or request in plain English. Press Enter or click the send button.

3

Read and refine

ChatGPT responds. If the answer isn't quite right, type a follow-up: "Can you make it shorter?" or "Explain it more simply."

4

Be specific for better results

Instead of "help me write an email," try "write a professional email to my landlord asking to delay rent payment by 2 weeks due to a work delay."

What AI Can Help You With

✓
Writing: Draft emails, letters, CVs, cover letters, essays, and business documents
✓
Explaining: "Explain how compound interest works as if I'm 10 years old"
✓
Summarising: Paste a long document and ask "summarise this in 5 bullet points"
✓
Translating: Translate text between any major languages
✓
Brainstorming: "Give me 10 name ideas for a small food delivery business"
✓
Coding help: Explain error messages, write simple scripts, debug code

What AI Cannot Do — Important Limitations

LimitationWhat This Means
Can make mistakes ("hallucinations")AI confidently gives wrong answers. Always verify important facts from official sources.
Knowledge cut-off dateAI may not know about very recent events
Cannot take real-world actionsIt can't send emails, make purchases, or access your accounts on its own
No personal memory by defaultEach new conversation starts fresh — it doesn't remember you
Cannot replace professionalsAlways consult a real doctor, lawyer, or financial advisor for serious decisions

Never enter sensitive personal information into AI tools: passwords, bank account numbers, passport numbers, or medical details. Treat AI chatbots like you would a public website — don't share anything you wouldn't want others to potentially see.

📌 Key Takeaways

✓
AI tools like ChatGPT are free and powerful helpers for writing, explaining, and brainstorming
✓
Be specific in your requests — more detail = better results
✓
AI can make confident mistakes — always verify important facts
✓
Never enter passwords, bank details, or ID numbers into AI tools
